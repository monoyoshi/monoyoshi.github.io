Welcome to the Hyperfixation Corner

Hey, congrats! You found the Hidden Treasure of Area 906! (I mean, I guess it's not really "hidden" per se...)


Anyway, the truth is, I mostly do everything as a hobby. My special interests are what fuels and motivates me, so I make things that tickle the silly autistic brain of mine, ranging from a simple piece of artwork to a straight-up mock website.


As a web developer, I specialize in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (JQuery included). I focus on these because of how easily accessible they are, and because I can't justify buying a webhosting service for myself.


As a graphic-design-leaning artist (hey, it really is my passion!), I enjoy making images that work very well when displayed on its own, whether it be as a small component for a bigger project or as a physical item like a shirt design.



VQUBER

"Remember Marth from the hit game Dragalia Lost? This is him now."
Main page

Originally known as Project DROCSI, this has been my passion project ever since Dragalia Lost announced the event Fire Emblem: Lost Heroes in April 2019. It stood for DRagalia Lost Original Character Self Insert (yeah yeah, it's goofy, but it stuck at the time). The character themselves prefer to go by the name REN.


Initially inspired by Marth Fire Emblem (and later on, Farren Dragalia Lost), REN is a character that represents most if not all of me. They're a monstrous fusion of both dragon and human who just wants to have fun.

TOU Café

"i am shef (also my gf)"
Main page

Tired of seeing something that looks delicious, only to find out it was made with nuts? Do you ever want to just easily access recipes without all the flashy clutter? Well, look no further than TOU Café: a project that achieves that and more!


This project comes from the fact that my girlfriend and I have a few Discord rooms dedicated to writing down recipes. You never know when Discord will go explosion emoji 💥, so I had the big brain idea of moving it all to a place that's much more easy to navigate.

hotaru(n)

"hahahahaha"
GitHub repository

A Discord bot that does a lot, from unserious things like laughing at people, to serious things like calculating the 36th number in the Fibonacci sequence (it's 14930352). hotaru(n) is also the predecessor to some of the other projects I have here. In a sense, he's like my sandbox...if sandboxes could be sassy and crave world domination. He is also my first-born, so he's riddled with a lot of curses. He doesn't mind, though.


Made using Javascript (Node.js) on a boring after-school November afternoon in 2016, hotaru(n) uses Discord.js to interact with Discord's API. And no, you can't add him onto your server yourself. I mean, unless you clone his code, but like, why? Also I am not liable for any damages he might cause. He's a mischievous little guy, I can tell you that.
